  • Credit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, Discover, American Express)

*Orders will ship within one business day of cleared payment. Payment by PayPal or credit card is generally instant. E-Checks via PayPal will take several days to clear, and orders will be shipped within one business day of receiving a cleared check notification.

Sales Tax:

  • Ebay directly collects the sales tax depending on your location

Shipping:

  • We ship with UPS and USPS. Any offer of "Free Shipping" is valid only in the continental United States.
  • When your order clears, you should receive an automated message with your tracking information. If you do not see this message within 24 hours of placing your order, feel free to contact us for this information.

Order Processing Time:

  • We will ship the same business day if you complete your purchase by 08:00 AM EST. All others will ship the next business day.
  • All orders ship within 24 hours!

Returns:

  • All of our products can be returned for FREE within 30 business days of receipt.
  • The item(s) must be returned in the original box in the exact NEW condition it was received.
  • Please pack the return properly so that it will arrive to us without damage.
  • Such returns must be shipped back to Cyber Mechanical and the correct return address will be provided by us upon accepting the return and we will provide a pre-paid shipping label
  • All items will be inspected upon return. If the part does not arrive in acceptable condition including scratches, dents, broken or missing parts, excessively dirty, or improperly packed causing damage in transit, then a restocking fee will be assessed and deducted from the refund.
